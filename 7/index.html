Pune Institute of Computer Technology, is a private unaided engineering college located in Ohankawadi. Pune, India. Established by the Society for Computer Technology and Research, SCTR in 1983. It offers degrees in Information Technology, Computer Engineering and Electronics and Telecommunication Engineering.
